THE SYMBOL LIBRARY

The same signs, in film after film, generation after generation

One symbol in one film is a curiosity. The same symbol in fifty films is a pattern. The same symbol in fifty films across one hundred years, planted in front of every child who watches a screen, is a discipleship program.

This library catalogs the recurring symbols that appear across modern film — not in one or two productions, but everywhere. Each symbol has a meaning rooted in real-world religious and occult tradition, and each is addressed somewhere in Scripture. The films are listed underneath as evidence that the symbol is not isolated — it is being placed, deliberately and consistently, in front of viewers from cradle to adulthood.

Read these and you will not watch a movie the same way again. That is the point.
